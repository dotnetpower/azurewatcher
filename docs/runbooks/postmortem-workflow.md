# Postmortem Workflow Runbook

Use this template after service recovery is verified and before incident closure.

## Procedure

1. Generate the draft from incident and append-only audit records.
2. Verify impact, chronology, RCA citations, actions, approvals, rollback, and recovery.
3. Separate root cause, contributing factors, and detection or response gaps.
4. Record what worked and what failed without editing machine records.
5. Assign corrective and preventive actions with owners and measurable evidence.
6. Submit reusable rule, runbook, or knowledge candidates through normal review.
7. Link the approved postmortem and close the incident.

## Stop conditions

Do not close when impact, recovery, unresolved risk, owner, or required follow-up
is missing. Keep unsupported causes labeled as hypotheses.
